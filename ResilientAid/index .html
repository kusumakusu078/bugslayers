🌊 Flooding & Water-Logging
  • Immediately switch off main circuit breakers.
  • Move to higher floors; never walk through moving water.
  • Avoid touching electrical appliances while standing in water.
  • Signal for help from windows or rooftops using cloth or torch.
🔥 Fire & Smoke
  • Stay low — smoke rises. Crawl to exits.
  • Close doors to slow fire spread; don't lock them.
  • Never use lifts during a fire.
  • Signal from windows; don't jump unless fire is at your door.
🏥 Medical — CPR Basics
  • Check responsiveness; shout for help.
  • 30 chest compressions at ~100/min, 2 rescue breaths.
  • Continue until help arrives or person recovers.
