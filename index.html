Drinking Water (20L) Sector 4
First Aid Kit Block B
🌊 FLOODING & WATER-LOGGING
  • Immediately switch off main circuit breakers to prevent electrocution.
  • Move valuables and essential documents to higher floors.
  • Do not walk or drive through moving water (6 inches can knock you down).
  • If trapped in a building, move to the roof—do not climb into a closed attic.
🔥 FIRE & GAS LEAKS
  • Stop, Drop, and Roll if clothing catches fire.
  • Stay low to the ground to avoid smoke inhalation.
  • If you smell gas, do NOT flip any light switches or use phones inside.
  • Check doors for heat with the back of your hand before opening.
🏚️ EARTHQUAKE / BUILDING COLLAPSE
  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On: Under a heavy table or desk.
  • Stay away from glass windows, mirrors, and hanging objects.
  • If outside, move to an open area away from power lines and buildings.
  • If trapped in debris, tap on a pipe or wall so rescuers can hear you.
🌪️ CYCLONE / SEVERE STORMS
  • Close and secure all windows and doors.
  • Stay in the strongest part of the house (usually an internal bathroom).
  • Keep a steady supply of drinking water stored in clean containers.
  • Beware of the 'Eye of the Storm'—the calm is temporary.
🩹 POST-DISASTER PROTOCOL
  • Check yourself and neighbors for injuries.
  • Listen to the ResilientAid Feed for official rescue updates.
  • Photograph property damage for insurance/government aid.
  • Boil all tap water before drinking until officially cleared.
